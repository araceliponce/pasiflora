The best way to control pests is to prevent them from getting on your plants in the first place. Inspect new plants carefully before bringing them indoors and isolate them from other plants for a few days to ensure they're pest-free. If you do have pests, try using insecticidal soap or neem oil, which are both safe and effective for indoor plants.
